Wel-come to Techin Recruitment Portal!

'The easiest way to get your new job.'

How It Works?

Step 1
Create Account
Go to Register page then fill the form and Click on Signup button to create an account.
Step 2
Activate your Account
Activate your account by going to email account you provided during signup.
Step 3
Create your Profile
After activating your account, you will be redirected to application page so fill the application form and submit it.
Optional Step
Update your Profile
After Submitting the application form it will be redirected you to your profile page where you can add additional information.
Step 4
Apply for Job
Click on Application List from Navigation bar to choose the application you want to apply on and then click Apply under action column
Step 5
Your Application Status
Finally, click on Application Status from Navigation bar to see the progress of your application.
